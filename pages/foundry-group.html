Meet The BitFoundry Group

We are passionate about freedom, adventure, science, art, software & clean technology.

The Bit Foundry

The Bit Foundry is home to various software products and services. The Bit Foundry is a software first foundry that supports products, clients and foundry partners with their AI, data, software and technology requirements.

Aerotechnical Foundry

The Aerospace & Aerotechical Foundry is working on electric aircrafts and VTOL jet concepts as well as air transport logistics solutions and booking systems. The Aerosport Foundry is also a function of the Aerotechnical Foundry and provides products & services pertaining to aerosports & recreation. Some products include sports management systems and indoor skydiving wind tunnel technology.

Clean Tech Foundry

The Clean Tech Foundry is passionate about energy innovation, sustainable design and building systems and solutions that optimize agriculture, health and energy efficiency. Together with our partners we prioritize solar and off-grid projects.

Paradise Foundry

The Paradise Foundry is a creative collective playing at the nexus of Art & Science. Working in the realm where logic and creativity meet our creative team takes the lead on all things art, design, production, writing, marketing, advertising and media distribution.

Scale up Foundry

The Scale up Foundry supports entreprenuers and SMEs via strategic partnerships, cutting edge web and software solutions, hackathons and events.We help businesses scale.

Blockchain Foundry

The Blockchain Foundry works with corpoates, governemnt and institutions to develop blockchain solutions that improve transparency, security and accessibility across various domains.
